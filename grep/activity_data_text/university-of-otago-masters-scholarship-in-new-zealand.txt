Eligibility Criteria :
If you want to study in New Zealand on a postgraduate scholarship, then you must apply for the University of Otago Coursework Master’s Scholarship 2025. However, before you do, make sure to go through the eligibility criteria for the Otago Scholarships in New Zealand below.
Eligibility Criteria for University of Otago Master’s Scholarship 2025:
The Otago Master’s Scholarship is open to both local and international students from all over the world.
Applicants must intend to study full-time in the first year of a master’s coursework degree program that does not include a thesis.
Or opting for the research report or dissertation pathway within a master’s program that also offers a thesis option.
Individuals who have previously completed a coursework master’s degree at the University of Otago are not typically eligible for this Master’s Scholarship in New Zealand.
Moreover, applicants must be enrolled in a coursework Master’s program, excluding the Master of Business Administration (MBA) unless it is solely paper-based. If research is involved, the research paper must be valued at less than 90 points.
The University of Otago Masters Scholarship 2025 in New Zealand is a highly competitive scholarship, and selection depends upon academic achievement and merit.
The Otago scholarship is for one year only, providing support for the applicants’ academic activities during this period.
All applicants must utilize this New Zealand scholarship within the year it is awarded.
In addition, the Otago Scholarship does not accommodate part-time study arrangements.
Recipients have the flexibility to hold the scholarship alongside other scholarships or awards they may receive, allowing for additional financial support during their studies.

Benefits :
Do you meet the eligibility criteria above? If yes, do not forget to read the various benefits of the Coursework Master’s Scholarship by University of Otago in New Zealand 2025. The list of benefits is compiled below.
Benefits of the University of Otago Masters Scholarship 2025:
The scholarship provides significant financial coverage of NZ$10,000 towards the fee in the first instance, with any remaining amount to be distributed as a monthly stipend throughout the duration of the scholarship.
The scholarship recognizes academic excellence and provides support to deserving students who demonstrate excellent academic achievements.
Similarly, by reducing financial constraints, the scholarship enables students to focus on their studies full-time. Hence, maximizing their academic potential and opportunities for learning and growth.
The Master’s scholarship serves as an incentive for students to pursue coursework master’s degrees at the University of Otago.
All students will get the chance to study in New Zealand with the Otago Scholarship.

Application Process:
Want to know how to apply for the University of Otago Masters Scholarships 2025 in New Zealand?
All interested applicants must apply online through the official website or click on the ‘apply now’ button below.
All applicants must complete an application form, which should accompany their application for admission to their chosen degree program.
Due to the limited availability of University of Otago Coursework Master’s Scholarship, invitations to apply are restricted by a nomination system.
Similarly, the University of Otago Scholarships Committee evaluates applicants depending on their academic record and the recommendation provided by the relevant Head of Department or nominee.
Once admitted to their program, the department will assess the suitability of candidates for scholarship funding and nominate them for consideration.
Eligible candidates can apply through eVision, and should have a GPA of at least an A on the most recent year of study as a requirement.
University of Otago Master’s Scholarship Deadline:
Applications for the University of Otago Masters Scholarships 2025 in New Zealand are initiated by departmental nomination and are currently open for the year 2025.
