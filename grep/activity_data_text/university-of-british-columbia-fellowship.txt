Eligibility Criteria :
Are you interested in pursuing a doctoral fellowship in Canada? If you are, make sure to go through the eligibility criteria for this fellowship program at University of British Columbia. Here are the eligibility criteria for the  British Columbia Fellowship.
Eligibility Criteria for University of British Columbia Fellowship 2025:
Both international and domestic students can apply for this international fellowship program in Canada.
Usually, students are offered the Four Year Fellowships in their first year of full-time DMA, PhD or MD-PhD degrees or continuing students.
However, the university may provide lesser funding in some situations.
Applicants must have an excellent academic record and must maintain their record to continue the UBC 4YF program.
Moreover, students getting any of the following Tri-agency awards will become designates of the Four Year Fellowship program:
NSERC Doctoral Postgraduate Scholarships (PGSD)
CIHR Doctoral Research Awards
SSHRC Doctoral Fellowships
Doctoral Canada Graduate Scholarships (CGSD)
Vanier Scholarships
Holders of any of the above scholarships might be eligible for 4YF tuition fee coverage and will get stipend and tuition assistance.
Selection is based on academic excellence and the recommendation of the graduate program.

Benefits :
Do you meet the eligibility criteria for this fellowship in Canada? If yes, you would not want to miss this opportunity. Keep on reading the various benefits of the University of British Columbia PhD Fellowship for international students.
Benefits of UBC Doctoral Fellowship in Canada 2025:
Recipients of the 4YF fellowship receive an annual stipend of $18,200, providing financial stability throughout their doctoral studies.
Fellows will also get financial assistance with full tuition fee coverage.
Moreover, the fellowship program in Canada aims to attract and support exceptional doctoral candidates, both domestically and internationally.
By providing stable base-level funding, the UBC fellowship ensures that students can focus on their research and academic pursuits without worrying about financial constraints.
It is also a great opportunity to study free in Canada and pursue research at one of the top universities in Canada.

Application Process:
Want to know how to apply for the Four Year Fellowship program? visit the official website or click on the ‘apply now’ button below.
All students must submit an admission application to the suitable graduate program at the University of British Columbia.
Students who receive offer of admission will automatically be considered for the University of British Columbia Fellowship in Canada.
Similarly, make sure you meet the eligibility criteria along with any additional requirements specific to the degree program.
Also, submit any necessary documents as per the program requirements.
UBC automatically considers students offered admission for this program’s funding.
However, make sure to apply before the application deadline.
The deadline is different for each program, so do check the website for more details.
Application Deadline for University of British Columbia Fellowship:
The deadline to apply for the University of British Columbia varies. However, it is likely in December 2024.
