Eligibility Criteria for the Strathclyde Business School Masters Scholarships 2025:
Applications for the Strathclyde Business School Masters Scholarships are open to all international students.
Applicants should be deemed international with regards to fee status.
International students must be self-funded (i.e. should not receive any funding from other scholarships, employer sponsorship, etc.)
Furthermore, applicants must be offered a place in one of the full-time MSc programs for the September 2025 intake.
Online and blended learning programmes are not eligible for this scholarship.
The University of
Strathclyde Scholarships are offered on a rolling basis. Hence, all applicants must apply before the deadline to be eligible for this international scholarship in UK 2025.

Benefits :
Ready to start your application process for this scholarship for international students at University of Strathclyde? Before doing so, read the details about the benefits and financial support offered by this postgraduate scholarship in UK for international students.
Benefits of the Strathclyde Business School Masters Scholarships in UK
:
International students will receive a scholarship between £6,000 and £8,000, depending on the course fee and subject area.
Moreover, through the Strathclyde Business School Masters Scholarships, applicants will get a chance to study for free in the UK.
Through this international scholarship, students will get an opportunity to make networks and connections globally.
This UK postgraduate scholarship is an amazing learning and research opportunity for master’s students.
Applicants will also have the opportunity to live and study in the United Kingdom and explore its culture.

Application Process:
Want to know how to apply for the Strathclyde Business School Masters Scholarships?
The application process for the Strathclyde Business School Scholarship is online. However, all applicants must meet the eligibility criteria and must receive a full-time offer to study MSc in September intake.
Click the ‘Apply Now’ button to go to the official portal.
Moreover, the masters scholarship is awarded automatically, no separate application is needed.
Strathclyde
Scholarships will be awarded on a rolling basis from January 2025 onwards.
Strathclyde Business School Masters Scholarships Deadline:
The deadline to apply for the Strathclyde Business School Masters Scholarships for international students is
31 July 2025
(September 2025 intake).
