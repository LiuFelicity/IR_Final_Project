Country:
Bulgaria

Fully Funded

Eligibility Criteria :
Do you want to become an INSAIT fellow? If yes, you must apply for this graduate research fellowship. However, before you apply, go through the eligibility criteria below to make sure you meet all requirements.
Eligibility Criteria for INSAIT Graduate Research Fellowship 2025:
The INSAIT graduate fellowship is open to applicants from around the world.
Graduate students currently enrolled in or recently graduated from a Master’s program at an accredited university are eligible for this fellowship in Europe.
Moreover, applicants should have studied a STEM-related field such as computer science, mathematics, physics, or engineering.
The program encourages applications from underrepresented groups in STEM.
Fellows must deliver a project plan in collaboration with their mentor at the start of the program.
They should also submit a final report summarizing their work, formatted as a conference paper, with the goal of submission to an appropriate conference.
Similarly, they will have to give a final presentation as part of the INSAIT seminar series.
The Bulgaria fellowship will take place in-person and remote work is not possible.
Therefore, all participants should be physically present at the institute in Sofia, Bulgaria, for a minimum of 8 weeks and a maximum of 12 weeks.

Benefits :
If you meet the eligibility criteria above, you would not want to miss the INSAIT Graduate Fellowship 2025. Read the various benefits this research fellowship offers all fellows. Opportunities Circles has listed the advantages for you below.
Benefits of INSAIT Graduate Research Fellowship:
INSAIT GRF is a fully funded fellowship that provides generous benefits and financial assistance to fellows.
It offers a generous monthly stipend of 2000 EUR to help students cover their living expenses during the program.
The fellowship in Bulgaria also provides accommodation and covers the costs.
Moreover, the graduate research fellowship provides travel and visa support for fellows.
All fellows will gain research experience and mentorship from experienced faculty.
It is a fantastic opportunity to study and live in Sofia, Bulgaria and experience the European culture.

INSAIT Graduate Fellowship Requirements:
All documents must be submitted in PDF format as part of the application.
CV
Unofficial transcript or a master’s diploma from your university
Motivation letter (1 page)

The entire application process for INSAIT Graduate Research Fellowship 2025 is online.
Carefully fill out the application form and submit your documents as part of the process.
The Institute for Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence and Technology Fellowship will last between 8 to 12 weeks, with the exact duration agreed upon by the student and their mentor.
INSAIT Graduate Research Fellowship Deadline:
There is no deadline to apply for INSAIT Graduate Research Fellowship Program. Applications are accepted throughout the year.
