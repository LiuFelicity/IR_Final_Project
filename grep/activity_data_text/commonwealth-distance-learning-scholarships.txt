Eligibility Criteria :
Are you also interested in applying for the Postgraduate scholarship in the UK? If yes, do not miss this opportunity! However, before you proceed, check the eligibility criteria and requirements below.
Eligibility Criteria for the Commonwealth Distance Learning Scholarships 2025:
Candidates must be citizens, refugees or permanent residents from the commonwealth country to be eligible.
British-protected individuals can also apply.
Students must be permanent residents in a developing Commonwealth country.
Candidates are considered if they possess the first degree of at least upper second-class standard.
Students who cannot afford to study in the UK otherwise are encouraged to apply for the commonwealth distance learning scholarships.
Candidates must hold an offer to start their selected course of study.
They must provide one reference.

Benefits :
Commonwealth Distance Learning scholarships for international students is fully funded. It will cover all costs related to studying at one of the top-ranked universities in the UK. Further information about the financial coverage and benefits is provided in this section. Therefore, make sure to read it carefully without skipping any points.
Benefits of the Commonwealth Distance Learning Scholarships 2025-26:
Full tuition fee coverage for eligible courses.
Commonwealth will also provide study grants where required.
Opportunity to recognize your talent.
Opportunity to gain international experience.
Study at one of the top UK universities from your home.
No requirement to travel.

Documents Required for the Commonwealth Scholarship Program:
Candidate’s official transcripts
Copy of passport or national ID card
Minimum Two references
Candidate’s curriculum Vitae (CV)/Resume

Application Process:
The application process is online. Click on the
apply now
button below.
Candidates have to apply online via the CSC application system. These scholarships are for September/October 2025.
Register yourself on the application system.
Then you will receive a key code from the CSC through an email.
Candidates must provide all required personal information, e.g., name, address, country, religion, etc.
Candidates have to provide official transcripts.
Upload a copy of the transcripts.
Furthermore, students have to provide the names of references.
Complete all the required fields that are required in the application.
Make sure to complete your application carefully and submit it.
After successfully submitting the commonwealth distance learning scholarship application, you will receive a confirmation email.

The Commonwealth Distance Learning Scholarships deadline is
