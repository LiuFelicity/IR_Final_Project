Eligibility Criteria for the Weidenfeld Hoffmann Scholarships 2025:
You must apply to the University of Oxford for a new graduate studies program.
Students presently enrolled at Oxford University are not eligible to apply for this Weidenfeld Oxford University scholarship.
Candidates must be a resident of the listed countries only to be eligible for this
Oxford Weidenfeld Hoffmann Scholarships and Leadership Program 2025.
After completing their degree, students must return to their home country.
Successful applicants must desire to help the global community through their chosen profession.
Candidates should be able to exhibit the link between their subject of study and long-term career objectives and how they envision their professional contribution to the betterment of their home country.
Weidenfeld-Hoffman scholars from Oxford University are also eligible to apply.
Applicants should return to their country of ordinary residence once their course is completed.

Benefits of the Oxford Weidenfeld-Hoffmann Scholarships and Leadership Programme in UK:
The Oxford-Weidenfeld and Hoffmann Scholarship and Leadership Programme will pay for all university and college expenses.
Candidates from emerging and low-economic countries are encouraged to apply for the W
eidenfeld Hoffmann scholarship.
Students will receive a 100% fee waiver.
They will also receive a living cost of £19,237.
The Oxford Hoffman scholarship award will be valid for the duration of fee liability for the agreed-upon course.
Candidates have an opportunity to learn about European culture and its history.
The Weidenfeld-Hoffmann scholarships University of Oxford offers the opportunity to study for free in the UK.

Application Process:
The application process for the University of Oxford Weidenfeld-Hoffmann Scholarships 2025 is online.
Students can apply through the official website by clicking the Apply Now button below.
There isn’t a specific separate application process for the scholarship.
To be considered for this Weidenfeld Hoffmann scholarship, applicants select the Weidenfeld-Hoffmann Scholarships and Leadership Programme in the Scholarships section of the University of Oxford graduate application form.
Complete all the required fields, including personal information, academic record, professional and volunteer experience, and other requirements.
Make sure to complete and upload the Weidenfeld-Hoffmann Scholarships Statement.
Upload the required documents in PDF format.
According to the course deadline, students must submit their complete application for the Oxford University
Weidenfeld Hoffmann Scholarships in UK
by December 2024 or January 2025.
Weidenfeld-Hoffmann Scholarships 2025 Deadline:
The
Weidenfeld Hoffmann Scholarships deadline
is between
December 2024 and January 2025
, according to the course deadline.
If you will be shortlisted for the
Weidenfeld Hoffmann scholarship
, applicants will be required to participate in the scholarship interview, held online in April 2025.
