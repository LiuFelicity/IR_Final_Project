Title: OYW Holcim Building Tomorrow Scholarship 2025 in Germany | Fully Funded - Opportunities Circle

Country:
Germany

Financial coverage:
Fully Funded

Deadline:
May 27, 2025

Eligibility Criteria :
The One Young World Summit is an annual summit for young leaders, and if you want to be a part of this annual summit, you should meet the eligibility criteria of the One Young World Holcim Scholarship.
Eligibility Criteria of the OYW Holcim Building Tomorrow Scholarship 2025:
Individuals from all over the world can apply.
They should be between 18 to 30 years old.
Should have a record of making a positive impact in one of the three focus areas of this Holcim Building Tomorrow Scholarship: Deploying AI technologies for sustainable construction, Employing AI solutions for urban resilience, and Responsible deployment of artificial intelligence in urban development.

Benefits :
Before starting the application process, you must check what benefits this international scholarship offers. One Young World Scholarships are fully funded scholarships that cover all expenses. We have listed the benefits below.
Benefits of OYW Holcim Building Tomorrow Scholarship 2025:
Access to the One Young World Summit 2025 in Munich, Germany from 3-6 November 2025.
Free air travel from their home country to Munich and vice versa.
Free hotel accommodation during the OYW Summit period (2 and 6 November 2025).
All meals will be provided, including breakfast, lunch, and dinner.
Fellows will also get ground transportation between Summit venues in Munich.
Participants will also attend the pre-program in Munich.
The One Young World Summit Scholarship 2025 is an opportunity to visit Germany for free.

Application Process:
Click the “Apply Now” button below to access the official OYW Holcim Scholarship website.
