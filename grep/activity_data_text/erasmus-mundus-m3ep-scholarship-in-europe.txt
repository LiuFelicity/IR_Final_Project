Eligibility Criteria :
Are you interested in applying for the Erasmus Mundus Scholarship? Before starting the application, you must check the M3EP Scholarship criteria. Opportunities Circle has listed the requirements below.
Eligibility Criteria for Erasmus Mundus M3EP Scholarship 2025/27:
Students from all over the world are eligible to apply for the Erasmus M3EP Program.
Applicants should have completed their bachelor’s degree or are in their final semester of the bachelor’s degree program.
They must fulfill the bachelor’s degree program requirements and admission requirements of the 5 partner institutions.
They must be proficient in the English language (IELTS, TOEFL, CAE, CPE).
They should meet the Erasmus Mundus Mobility requirements.
Applicants must be full-time students enrolled in a master’s degree program.

Benefits :
Before starting your application for this fully funded European scholarship, make sure to check the benefits and financial coverage of this Erasmus Mundus M3EP Scholarship below.
Benefits of Erasmus Mundus Joint Master’s Scholarship 2025:
Students will get a full tuition fee waiver.
There is no application or registration fee.
The scholars will get an allowance of 33,600 € (1400 € per month for 2 years).
The allowance can be used to cover living expenses, including travel, visa, accommodation, and other study-related expenses.
A chance to visit Europe for free.
Fully funded opportunity to explore the European culture and learning environment.

Erasmus Mundus M3EP Scholarship 2025 Requirements:
Bachelor’s Degree Transcript
Self-assessment form
Course descriptions of courses
Curriculum Vitae (CV)
GPA
English language proficiency
Copy of passport or l ID
Letter of motivation
Proof of your current country of residence
Official bachelor’s diploma

Application Process:
The application process for the Erasmus Mundus scholarship is online.
How to apply for Erasmus Mundus M3EP Scholarship in Europe:
Applicants can apply through the online Application Portal available on the official website.
They will be required to create an account on the portal.
After creating an account, start their application.
Make sure to add all the asked details correctly.
Upload all the required documents.
Make sure to select the option to apply for the Erasmus Mundus Joint Mater’s degree scholarship.
Review and submit your application before the Erasmus scholarship deadline.
Keep visiting the admissions portal to check the results of your scholarship application.
Note:
Use Firefox or Google Chrome as a browser to fill out, submit, and access your application.
Erasmus Scholarship Deadline:
The last date to apply for the Erasmus Mundus M3EP Scholarship 2025 in Europe is
15th January 2025
for non-EU students, and
1st March 2025
for EU students.
