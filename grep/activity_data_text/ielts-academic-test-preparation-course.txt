Eligibility Criteria for Free IELTS Test Preparation Courses by the University of Queensland:
Anyone belonging to any country, age, religion, or race can apply for
the University of Queensland’s free online courses.
Applicant must have the latest browser version.
The online preparation course is totally free, but if you want a verified certificate, then you will have to pay.
Applicant must have a strong internet connection.
Must have an interest in learning.
Anyone can enroll in the course.

Application Process:
The application procedure is online. Applicants must create an online account following the steps below:
Enter your email address.
Enter your name; remember, this name will be used for your certificate.
Create a public username.
The username cannot be changed.
Create a password and add your country.
Finally, click Create an account.
Now, you can choose your desired course and click enroll.
Once you’re enrolled for any free course, you can start the f
ree IELTS Preparation Course 2024 right away!
IELTS Free Online Courses Deadline:
There is no deadline for the IELTS Academic Test Preparation Course by the University of Queensland, and you can enroll anytime.
