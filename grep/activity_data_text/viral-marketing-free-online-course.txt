Are you also interested in learning to make contagious content and do viral marketing to reach out to potential costumers and clients? Register for the free online course to learn more about viral marketing, but before that, read the criteria.
Eligibility Criteria for Viral Marketing Free Course:
The free online course on marketing is open for all countries.
There is no gender, nationality or educational background restrictions.
Individuals from any academic background can register for this viral marketing course.
You must have interest to learn about marketing and creating contagious content.

Application Process:
Visit the
Apply Now
link to register for the free marketing course.
First of all, you must create an account on the learning platform or log in to an existing account.
Select “Enroll for Free” and start learning.
You can start the course after selecting the module/lesson.

How to apply for financial aid for the free course?
Click on the Financial Aid option next to the Enroll for Free option.
Select the course you want to apply for financial aid.
Applicants must answer a few questions.
The financial aid application review will take up to 16 days, and then the applicant will receive a response about the financial aid status.
Viral Marketing Free Online Course Deadline:
