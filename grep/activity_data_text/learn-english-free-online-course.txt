Eligibility Criteria :
If you want to learn the basic of English language, you must enroll for the Learn English Free Online Course. However, before applying, go through the eligibility criteria and requirements of this course below.
Eligibility Criteria for Learn English Free Online Course
Applicants must have access to the internet to access the English course.
Applicants from around the world, with any nationality can enroll for this course.
It is a beginner-level course and there are no prerequisites.
Moreover, the course is open to everyone; students, learners and even professionals can register.

Benefits :
Do you meet the eligibility criteria above? If yes, you would not want to miss this opportunity to learn English. Keep on reading to know more about the benefits of enrolling in the online English language course.
Benefits of Learn English Free Online Course:
The course is available to everyone and there is no previous experience requirement.
Learners will explore key aspects of English grammar, including word forms, verb tenses, and question and answer formation, helping them begin their English learning journey effectively.
Learners can study at their own pace.
Moreover, with exercises and quizzes, learners will apply what they have learned in real-world contexts.
It is a beginner-level course and those who want to learn English can polish their language skills by sitting at home.
It is a high rated course on Coursera and is one of the Top free English language courses.
Applicants will be able to use English grammar with confidence by the end of the specialization course.

How to Apply for Financial Aid for the Learn English Free Online Course?
Learners can apply for financial aid to access the Learn English Beginning Grammar Specialization for free and earn a certificate.
Fill out the financial aid form to request free access to the course.
Select “Learn English: Beginning Grammar Specialization” when submitting the application.
Click “Next” and enter the required information.
Ensure the application contains at least 150 words explaining the need for financial aid.
The review process usually takes approximately 16 days.
