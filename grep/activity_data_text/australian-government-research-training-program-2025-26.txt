Eligibility Criteria :
Are you looking for Australia scholarships? If yes, then this RTP scholarship in Australia is for you. Before starting the application, check the Australian Government Research Training Program requirements. Details are listed in this section.
Eligibility Criteria Australian Government Scholarship 2025:
Applicants must be enrolled at an
eligible Australian university
to be eligible for the Australian Government RTP 2025.
Applicants must be enrolled in a full-time Higher Degree by Research (HDR) program at a participating university.
Applicants must also fulfill the eligibility criteria of the participating university to which they are applying.
International applicants must provide evidence of having the minimum English language proficiency requirements for applying for the RTP scholarship.
Applicants for the Australian Government Research Training Program Stipend must not receive an equivalent award, scholarship, or salary to undertake the proposed degree (equivalent being greater than 75% of the base stipend).
International applicants are required to pay any visa application fees and requirements for the Australian scholarship.
It is the responsibility of the candidate to ensure that they hold a valid visa while in Australia.
Applicants must meet the eligibility criteria for the
Research Training Program RTP listed in the
Commonwealth Scholarships Guidelines
(Research).

Benefits :
Ready to start the application process for an Australian Government research scholarship? Before that, check the benefits and financial support of the Australian Government Research Scholarships 2025. Opportunities Circle has listed the benefits of the Australian Government Scholarship 2025 below.
Benefits of the Australian Government Research Training Program Scholarships (RTPS) 2025:
International Students will receive a stipend of 52,352 at max annually.
All international students receiving the Australian Research Scholarships
will have their tuition fees waived for the
standard duration of the RTP scholarship at any of the participating universities.
The Australia Research Scholarship will also provide RTP allowances to help students with the ancillary costs of an HDR (higher degree by research). These include relocation costs to undertake an HDR, thesis printing costs, and academic publication costs.
Furthermore, AGRTP will also include health cover costs for recipients and their dependents.
International applicants will get a networking opportunity.
The
Australian Government Research Scholarship 2025 is an opportunity to study in Australia for free.

Application Process:
The application process for the fully funded Research Training Program Scholarship in Australia is online.
How to Apply for the Australian Government Research Training Program 2025-26?
Applicant must check his eligibility before starting the Research Training Program (RTP) scholarship applications.
The Australian Government Research Program includes different participating universities.
Each university has its own application process.
Applicants have to select one of the
eligible universities
that fit their interests.
After selecting the university, they must visit the official website of that higher education provider (university) to get more details regarding the application process.
Applicants must fulfill the requirements of that university.
The RTP Scholarship Australia deadline varies, so make sure to check the deadline of the university before starting the Australian Research Training Scholarship 2025 application process.
AGRTPS 2025 Deadline:
The
deadline for the
Australian Government Research Training Program Scholarships (RTPS)
2025-26 is not
specified.
Every university has its own application deadline, so
the applicants must visit the official website of the
selected university to get more details.
