Eligibility Criteria :
If you want to learn the basics of Spanish, you must apply for this Spanish for Beginners Free Online Course. However, before you apply, go through the eligibility criteria below to ensure you meet all requirements.
Eligibility Criteria for Free Spanish Language Course:
Applicants must have access to the internet to enroll in this online Spanish course.
They should have an interest in learning the language.
Learners from all around the world can apply for the Basic Spanish 1 course.
There are no prerequisites since it is an introductory level course for beginners.
Moreover, learners should be ready spend 4-5 hours per week.

Benefits :
Do you meet the eligibility criteria above? If yes, you would not want to miss this Spanish for beginners course. Keep on reading to know more about the benefits this online course offers all candidates. Opportunities Circle has listed the benefits below.
Benefits of Free Spanish Language Course:
The course offers flexibility and learners can progress at their own pace.
It is an introductory level course, specifically for English speakers.
Similarly, the Spanish for Beginners free online course will help learn conversational Spanish, introducing students to basic skills and everyday language.
Learners will be able to build confidence by mastering a new skill and engaging in conversations.
It is an excellent opportunity to learn Spanish and connect with millions of Spanish speakers worldwide.

Application Process:
Want to know how to learn Spanish for free? Click the ‘apply now’ button below to enroll for the Free Spanish Language Course.
However, before you apply, make sure you meet the eligibility criteria and requirements for this course.
Candidates can enroll through the audit course option or pay a small amount and earn a certificate.
The application process is online and learners must create an account before the can start learning.
Make sure to check the ending date of the course since it will be archived after that.
Free Spanish Language Course Deadline:
There is no deadline to apply for the Free Spanish Language Course 2025.
