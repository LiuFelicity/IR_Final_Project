Kyushu University was founded in 1911 and has grown into one of the most prestigious universities in Japan. At present, there are 12 undergraduate schools, 18 graduate schools, 4 research institutes and a hospital.
Approximately 19,000 students, 2,100 faculty members, and 2,300 administrative staff members are engaged in study and / or research at the University. Kyushu University is located in Fukuoka, the gateway to Kyushu.
Geographically and historically, the city is close to other parts of Asia, so we have aimed to be the “University open to Asia” from the beginning. In addition, a new main campus was opened in 2005, in Ito, where some departments have started their activities.
