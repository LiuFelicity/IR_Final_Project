Eligibility Criteria :
Are you interested in becoming a part of this summer exchange program? If you are, do not forget to go through the eligibility criteria for the Romania Bucharest Summer Exchange Program 2025 to ensure you meet all the requirements.
Eligibility Criteria for Bucharest Summer University 2025 in Romania:
Applicants can apply from any country.
Students pursuing bachelor’s, master’s, and PhD can apply for 2025 BSU.
Furthermore, the purpose of visiting Romania, the type of passport, and the country that issued the passport must match all the university requirements.

Benefits :
If you meet the eligibility criteria above, make sure to apply before the Bucharest Summer Exchange Program deadline. However, before you apply, you must read the various benefits of this exchange program 2025. .
Benefits of the Romania Bucharest Summer University 2025:
The program offers different types of scholarship. Full scholarship covers full program fee.
Three meals per day and accommodation.
Participants will also get transport to and from the airport.
They will also get a weekend trip (hotels, meals, transportation).
Academic courses.
Cultural exploration.
Recreational facility.
Interpersonal skill development.
Additionally, participants get to engage in social events.
Team-building skills development.
Network building.

BSU Summer Program Requirements:
Updated CV
Passport copy (if required)
Essay (2000 words)
Motivational letter

Application Process:
Click on the Apply Now button below to start your application.
How to Apply for the Bucharest Summer University in Romania?
Individuals have to apply through the official website.
Individuals need to fill in the application form first.
Make sure to apply for the full scholarship application to get this fully funded summer school. Applicants may still have to pay a nominal additional fee.
They must upload a resume (CV) and write an essay in the specified format.
Ensure to provide any other supporting documents.
Applicants must carefully complete and submit their application form.
Bucharest Summer University Deadline:
The application deadline for the Romania Bucharest Summer University 2025 is in
May 2025
.
