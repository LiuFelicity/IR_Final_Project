Eligibility Criteria :
You must be interested in applying for the Joint Japan World Bank Scholarship. Before you proceed to the application, check the World Bank Scholarship 2025 requirements. Students must meet the following criteria to apply.
Eligibility Criteria of the Joint Japan World Bank Scholarship Program 2025:
Applicants must have citizenship in one of the eligible countries. You can check the list
here
.
They must not have dual citizenship of any developed country.
They must have completed a Bachelor’s degree no less than three years before applying for the Joint Japan World Bank Graduate Scholarship Program 2025.
Applicants have at least three years of work experience after completing a Bachelor’s degree.
Applicants should have no major health issues to be eligible for the JJWBGSP 2025.
Furthermore, they must not have relatives (through blood, marriage, or adoption) who work for the World Bank.
Applicants should have employment in the development sector.
Moreover, a minimum of three years of employment in the development sector after completing an accredited bachelor’s degree is required.
Furthermore, applicants must meet the criteria of the JJ/WBGSP Participating Programs and universities to which they are applying.
When applying for the Joint Japan World Bank Scholarship Program, applicants must fulfill all eligibility criteria. The host organization will not consider applicants who do not meet all the requirements.
Applicants must be admitted unconditionally for the upcoming academic year in one of the JJWBGSP participating programs before the scholarship application deadline.
Individuals who have previously received a JJ/WBGSP scholarship but did not graduate or have previously been offered a Joint Japan scholarship but refused it, are not eligible to apply again.

Benefits :
Are you ready to apply for the JJWBGS? Hurry up and start the application process. The scholarship covers all expenses related to studying abroad. Opportunities Circle has listed these JJ/WBGSP scholarships benefits below.
Benefits of the Joint Japan World Bank Scholarship Program 2025:
Opportunity to study abroad and gain knowledge in the field of your interest.
The Joint Japan World Bank Scholarship 2025 will cover the cost of economy-class air travel.
$600 will be provided as a travel allowance for each trip.
In addition, JJ/WBGSP 2025 will provide full tuition fee coverage.
The JJWBGSP Scholarship for International Students will also cover the medical insurance of scholars.
Lastly, a monthly allowance will cover accommodation, food, and book costs.
The World Bank Scholarships Program is an amazing chance for you to study abroad for free.

Joint Japan World Bank Scholarship Program Requirements:
The following are the required documents for the Joint Japan World Bank Scholarships Program – JJ/WBGSP 2025:
Bachelor’s degree/diploma
Proof of work experience
Letter of admission
Two recommendation letters
Complete application form
Medical certificate
Valid passport/identification card
Selection Criteria for the Joint Japan World Bank Scholarship 2025:
Joint Japan World Bank Scholarship applicants will be assessed based on the following factors:
Quality of Work experience (30% weightage)
Quality of commitment to their home country (30% weightage)
Educational Background (10% weightage)
Professional Recommendations (30% weightage)
Note:
The Joint Japan World Bank Graduate Scholarship Program aims to ensure that the graduate scholarship recipients are equal to both genders and all eligible countries. The scholarship program also strives to ensure that all applicants’ special or typical conditions are considered, and it looks at all applicants’ financial strength.

Application Process:
The application process for the Joint Japan World Bank Graduate Scholarship Program is online.
How to Apply for the Joint Japan World Bank Scholarship Program 2025?
Applicants can apply for the scholarship through the World Bank’s official website or by clicking the Apply Now button below.
Upload all the required documents.
Two reference letters from the referees who know the applicant’s work experience are required.
Applicants must make sure to fill in the application carefully and submit it before the deadline.
The JJ/WBSP application deadline for window 2 is 25 March to 23 May 2025 (noon at Washington time).
