Eligibility Criteria :
Are you looking for a research scholarship to study free in Australia? If yes, then this fully funded scholarship in Australia is for you. Before starting the application, check the criteria for the University of Sydney Australian Government Research Training Program Scholarships (RTPS).
Eligibility Criteria University of Sydney RTP Scholarship 2026:
Applicants must be eligible to enroll in any
Higher Degree by Research
(HDR) program at the University of Sydney.
Applicants must be commencing or enrolled as an international postgraduate research student at the University of Sydney.
Applicants must have an unconditional offer letter or be currently enrolled in a master’s or PhD at the University of Sydney.
Applicants must not hold Australian permanent resident status, Australian citizenship, or New Zealand citizenship.
Applicants must fulfill the academic requirements of the University of Sydney for a master’s or Doctorate program.
Applicants must have completed their Bachelor’s Degree for a master’s program and master’s degree for a PhD program.
Applicants must fulfill the eligibility criteria of the Australian Government Research Training Program listed in the Commonwealth Scholarships Guidelines (Research).
Applicants must have an outstanding academic record and research potential.
Applicants must fulfill the English Language Requirements.

Benefits :
Ready to start the application of this research scholarship? Before that, check the benefits of the Australian Government Research Training Program Scholarships (RTPS) 2026. Opportunities Circle has listed the benefits of Australian Government RTPS 2026 below.
Benefits of the Australian Government Research Training Program Scholarships (RTPS) 2026:
International Students will receive Stipends of $41,753 per year.
The stipend will help students to cover their living costs.
All international students receiving the University of Sydney Research Scholarship will have their tuition fees waived for the standard duration of the scholarship.
International students will receive Overseas Student Health Cover (OSHC).
International students will get a networking opportunity.
A chance to study free in Australia.

Required Document for University of Sydney RTP Scholarship 2026:
Official Academic Transcripts
Curriculum Vitae (CV)
Research proposal
Acceptance Letter by supervisor
Proof of Identity
Two referee reports
Proof of English language proficiency
