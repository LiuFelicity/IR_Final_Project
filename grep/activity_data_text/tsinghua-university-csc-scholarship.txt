Eligibility Criteria :
Are you also applying for the fully funded Chinese Government Scholarship Program 2025? Before you start the application for the CSC Council scholarship, check the Tsinghua University CSC Scholarship 2025 requirements. Opportunities Circle has listed the criteria for the Tsinghua University Scholarship in China.
Eligibility Criteria for the Tsinghua University CSC Scholarship 2025 in China:
All international students are eligible for the CSC scholarship.
Applicants should have good physical and mental health.
Chinese residents are not eligible for this Tsinghua University Scholarship 2025.
Applicants of the Master’s program must have a bachelor’s degree.
The age limit for applicants to a master’s program is a maximum of 35 years at the time of application.
Applicants of the doctoral program must have a master’s degree.
The age limit for doctoral program applicants is a maximum of 40 years at the time of application.
At the time of application, applicants should not be enrolled in any other Chinese university.
Applicants should have excellent academic performance.
Applicants must meet the language requirements of the University.

Benefits :
Students must check the financial coverage of the Chinese scholarship they are applying for. The CSC scholarships in China for international students is a fully funded scholarship program funded by the government of China; therefore, all costs related to studying at Tsinghua University will be covered.
Benefits of the Tsinghua University CSC Scholarship 2025:
Complete Tuition Fee.
Living and accommodation expenses.
Medical insurance with specific limits will be provided.
Scholars will also get a monthly stipend.
Candidates will be promoted for the next academic year’s scholarship based on their performance in the previous academic year.
An opportunity to study free in China

Chinese Government Scholarship 2025 Requirements:
Applicants must submit the following material to apply for the Tsinghua University CSC Scholarship:
CGS Application Form (in Chinese or English)
ID Photo
A Scanned Copy of the Passport Information Page
Diploma and Certificate of Highest Education
Academic transcript
Valid Language Qualification Certificate
Pre-admission documents
A Study Plan or Research Proposal (in Chinese or English)
Two Recommendation Letters (in Chinese or English)
A Photocopy of the Foreigner Physical Examination Form (in Chinese or English)
Non-Criminal Record Report
Other Supporting Documents

Application Process:
Applicants can apply for the Tsinghua University CSC scholarship 2025 in China by clicking the Apply Now button below.
At first, applicants must apply for graduate programs at
Tsinghua University
to get pre-admission and consult referred schools/departments for CGS Type B application.
The deadline for some CGS Type B programs is 15 December 2024, so it is recommended that you apply before it. Otherwise, applicants can miss the deadline of some CGS Type B programs
Applicants complete their CGS Online Application form completely on the
CSC Online Application Portal
when informed by the pre-admitted schools/departments.
Tsinghua University selects CGS applicants based on recommendations of schools/departments, then further nominates them to the CSC for the fully funded Chinese Government Scholarship.
Applicants must upload all the required documents with the application form. Documents must be clear and valid. The required documents must be in English or Chinese language (for other languages, notarized Chinese or English translations must be attached).
All the CGS applications should meet the CSC scholarship requirements. Otherwise, the CGS application will not be processed.
For more information on the Tsinghua University CSC scholarship application, click the Apply Now button or visit the official website.
Tsinghua University Scholarship Deadline:
The last date to apply for the Tsinghua University CSC scholarship 2025 in China is different for every program. So make sure to check the deadline of the selected program before starting your application.
