Eligibility Criteria :
The One Young World Summit is an exciting opportunity for Philippians. Leaders who want to be a part of this fully funded annual summit must meet the eligibility criteria of the One Young World Scholarship.
Eligibility Criteria of the OYW Rappler Scholarship 2025:
Applicants must be nationals of the Philippines.
Applicants should be between 18 and 30 years old to be eligible (there may be an exception for those who have significant personal impact and leadership experience)
They should have demonstrated capacity for leadership.
Applicants should have a track record of creating positive impact in either anti-hate initiatives, or responsible technology focused initiatives.
Must have experience in volunteering.
They will be required to hold a community chat about any issue important to their group, sector, or community.

Benefits :
Before starting your application, you must check what benefits are offered by the Rappler scholarship. OYW Scholarship is a fully funded scholarship, and we have mentioned its benefits below.
Benefits of OYW Rappler Scholarship 2025 in Germany:
Full access to the One Young World Summit 2025 in Munich.
Free hotel accommodation is available during the OYW Summit period.
Free air travel to and from Munich.
All meals, including breakfast, lunch, and dinner, will be provided.
Fellows will also get ground transportation between the Summit venues in Munich.
The One Young World Summit Scholarship is an opportunity to visit Germany for free.
Opportunity to connect with other fellow delegates.
