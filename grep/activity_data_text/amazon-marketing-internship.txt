Eligibility Criteria :
Do you want to work with Amazon? If yes, you must apply for their Marketing Internship. However, before you do, make sure to go through the eligibility criteria below to check if you meet the requirements of this Germany Internship.
Eligibility Criteria for Amazon Marketing Internship 2025:
The Amazon Internship 2025 is open to students from around the world.
They must be currently pursuing a bachelor’s or master’s Degree, with graduation expected after the internship ends.
All interns should be available to start the internship between January and August 2025, with flexible start dates.
They should also commit to a full-time internship (40 hours/week) for a duration of three to six months, depending on availability.
Moreover, applicants should be proficient in written and spoken English (C1 level or higher).
Interns must be based in Munich or Hamburg for the internship, with a possible relocation package offered, subject to eligibility criteria.
According to the preferred qualifications, applicants should have studies within Business, Marketing, Economics, Engineering, or relevant fields.
Similarly, they should be proficient in MS Office programs (e.g., Excel, PowerPoint).
Interns should be fluent in written and spoken German (B2 level or higher). A virtual German language test may be required to assess proficiency. Applications with German proficiency at B1 level or lower will also be considered.

Benefits :
If you meet the eligibility criteria above, you would not want to miss this paid internship in Europe. Keep on reading to know more about the benefits this international internship offers students.
Benefits of Amazon Marketing Internship:
It is a paid internship for students. Hence, they do not have to worry about financial restrictions.
They will receive mentorship that aligns with their career goals.
All interns will have access to a wide range of virtual trainings, including project management, personal branding and communication skills.
Similarly, they will get exclusive Amazon.de discounts, a Deutschlandticket (transportation ticket), bike rental, gym membership discounts and opportunities to beta test new Amazon products.
They will also have access to free mental health, legal, and financial support.
Successful completion of the internship may open the door to a full-time graduate role after university.
The marketing internship will take place in Munich and Hamburg, and interns may receive a relocation package.

Application Process:
Want to know how to get an Internship at Amazon? Click the ‘apply now’ button below to start your application.
Before you apply, make sure to review the requirements and criteria.
All applicants must create an account on the official portal before applying for the Amazon Marketing Internship.
Similarly, the application takes under 10 minutes, requiring only a CV and answers to a questionnaire, no cover letter needed.
Selected candidates will complete an online assessment, followed by two rounds of virtual interviews on the same day.
Make sure to apply before the deadline.
Amazon Marketing Internship Deadline:
The deadline to apply for Amazon Marketing Internship is not mentioned. Make sure to apply as early as possible.
