Eligibility Criteria :
If you are interested in online courses and upskilling, you must apply for these FinTech Courses for beginners. However, before you enroll, make sure you meet the eligibility criteria and requirements below.
Eligibility Criteria for Introduction to FinTech Free Course:
It is an introductory-level course, open to everyone from around the world.
Applicants should spend one to three hours per week to complete the course.
No previous experience is required for the FinTech online course.
Similarly, all candidates must have access to the internet and a mobile device.
Candidates can enroll for free through the audit track. However, they will have to pay an additional amount if they wish to get a certificate.
There are no prerequisites of enrolling in this course.

Benefits :
Do you want to learn FinTech? If you do, you would not want to miss the HKUx: Introduction to FinTech. Continue reading to know more about the benefits you will get by enrolling in the free FinTech course.
Benefits of Introduction to FinTech Free Course:
Learners can learn at their own pace with this self-paced course.
They will understand the basic concepts of financial technology.
Applicants will learn skills such as financial technology, infrastructure, big data, AI, blockchain, finance and more.
Similarly, learners will learn from top experts and gain practical skills.
It is a fantastic opportunity to study FinTech and grow your career in the field.

Application Process:
Do you want to know how to apply for the Introduction to FinTech Free Course? Click the ‘apply now’ button below to start your application.
Make sure you meet the criteria and are eligible to enroll in this course.
All applicants must create an account on edX before they can register.
