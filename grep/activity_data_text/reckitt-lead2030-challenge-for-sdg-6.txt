Eligibility Criteria :
The One Young World Summit in Germany is a life-changing opportunity for young leaders. Those who want to be a part of this annual summit must meet the eligibility criteria for the One Young World Reckitt Lead2030 Scholarship.
Eligibility Criteria of the Reckitt Lead2030 Challenge for SDG 6:
The solution must be aligned with the SDG6 indicators.
It must be a youth-led initiative founded by a person aged 18 â€“ 30.
The solution is readily available, being piloted, implemented, or scaled.
It must have a clear implementation and scale-up plan.
Solutions must have a positive social impact, for example, generating educational outcomes, promoting health, or employment opportunities.
The impact of the solution on society must be adequately measured.
It must be financially viable and scalable.

Benefits :
Before starting your application, you must check what benefits are offered by the Reckitt Lead2030 Challenge. It is a fully funded scholarship, and we have mentioned its benefits below.
Benefits of the Reckitt Lead2030 Challenge for SDG 6:
The winning solution will receive a sponsorship to participate in the One Young World Summit 2025.
The winning solution will also get a US$50,000 grant from Reckitt.
Moreover, they will also get 12 months of free mentorship from a team of Reckitt professionals, which will help them accelerate their solutions based on the needs of their initiative or organization.
The 2025 SDG6 Lead2030 Challenge powered by Reckitt is an amazing opportunity to visit Germany for free.
