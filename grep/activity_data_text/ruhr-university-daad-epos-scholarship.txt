Eligibility Criteria :
There’s no better scholarship than the DAAD EPOS Scholarships for those who want to study free in Germany. However, before you start your application process, check the criteria for the fully funded EPOS scholarship below.
Eligibility Criteria for the Ruhr University DAAD EPOS Scholarship 2026:
This international scholarship is open to students from all over the world.
Applicants should have a desire to pursue their career in international cooperation and development cooperation.
They must have at least two years of relevant experience in the DAAD-EPOS Development Program.
They should fulfill the English proficiency requirement (TOEFL (79-80 iBT) or IELTS (6.0)).

Benefits :
Want to know more about the DAAD EPOS scholarship in Germany? Opportunities Circle has listed the benefits of this fully funded scholarship in Europe in this section.
Benefits of the Ruhr University DAAD EPOS Scholarship 2026 in Germany:
Students will get a monthly stipend of €992.
Health, accident, and liability coverage is provided.
Travel allowance will be provided.
Scholars will get a monthly rent subsidy.
Moreover, they can also get a monthly allowance for accompanying family members.
An opportunity to study free in Germany.

Ruhr University DAAD Scholarship Requirements:
Personally signed motivation letter for DAAD-EPOS postgraduate course.
A Curriculum Vitae (please use the Europass CV tool ).
Higher education degree certificate(s) and transcript(s) of records.
School leaving certificate.
Language certificate(s).
Evidence of current and former employment.
Two recommendation letters.
Copy of passport or identity card.
Passport size photo.
Completed and signed the
DAAD checklist
.
Completed the
DAAD application form
.

Application Process:
The application process for the Ruhr University DAAD EPOS Scholarship is online.
Click on the Apply Now button to visit the official website.
Create an account on the online
MADM Application Platform
.
Fill out the application form for the Master of Arts in Development Studies (MADM).
Upload all the required documents.
Review your application and submit it before the EPOS scholarship application deadline.
