Eligibility Criteria :
Here are the criteria for job opportunities for young individuals seeking to join the team:
Social Media Manager:
Maximum 28 years old.
Work experience as a social media manager or in a similar role.
In-depth knowledge of various social media platforms, features, and best practices.
Proficient in using social media management tools and analytics platforms.
Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
Strong creative and visual storytelling abilities.
Must be able to analyze data and derive insights to optimize social media strategies.
Strong organizational and multitasking skills.
Good command of Canva and creative designing skills; command of Photoshop will be a plus point.
Content Writer:
Excellent writing, editing, and proofreading skills.
Strong command of grammar, punctuation, and style.
Candidates must be able to adapt writing styles and tones for different platforms and target audiences.
Proficient in conducting research using reliable sources.
Familiarity with SEO principles and keyword optimization.
Creative thinking and ability to generate engaging ideas.
Strong attention to detail and ability to meet deadlines.
Assistant Business Development Manager:
Bachelor’s degree in business, marketing, or a related field.
Previous experience in a business development or sales support role is preferred.
Strong interpersonal and communication skills.
Excellent organizational and time management abilities.
Proficiency in Microsoft Office and other relevant software.
Analytical mindset with the ability to gather and interpret data.
Candidates must be able to work both independently and as part of a team.

Job/Internship Description :
Social Media Manager:
Develop and implement social media strategies and campaigns to increase brand awareness and engage with the target audience.
Create and curate compelling content for social media platforms, including text, images, and video content.
Manage and maintain social media accounts, including scheduling posts, monitoring engagement, and responding to comments or messages.
Analyze social media metrics and provide reports on key performance indicators (KPIs) to assess the effectiveness of campaigns.
Stay updated with social media trends and best practices, and suggest new ideas for social media marketing initiatives.
Collaborate with other departments, such as marketing and public relations, to ensure consistent messaging and brand representation.
Monitor and manage online reputation by addressing customer concerns or complaints on social media platforms.
Content Writer:
Write engaging and informative content for various platforms.
Conduct thorough research on industry-related topics to generate ideas and gather relevant information.
Write clear, concise, and compelling content that aligns with the brand voice and target audience.
Edit and proofread content.
Collaborate with team members to ensure content meets the desired objectives.
Optimize content for search engines (SEO) by incorporating relevant keywords and following SEO guidelines.
Stay updated with industry trends and content marketing best practices.
Assistant Business Development Manager:
Contact potential clients and convert leads into sales.
Maintain positive relationships with current clients.
