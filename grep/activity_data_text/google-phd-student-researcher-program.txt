Eligibility Criteria :
Do you want to become a Student Researcher? If you do, you must apply for this internship for students. However, before you apply, go through the eligibility criteria below to ensure you meet all requirements.
Eligibility Criteria for Google PhD Student Researcher Program:
The student researcher program is open to PhD Students.
They must be currently enrolled in a PhD program in a relevant field such as Computer Science, Linguistics, Statistics, Biostatistics, Applied Mathematics, Operations Research, Economics, Natural Sciences, or a related technical discipline.
Should have experience in at least one specialized area of computer science, such as Human Computer Interactions, Natural Language Understanding, Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Algorithmic Foundations of Optimization, Quantum Information Science, Data Science, Software Engineering or similar areas.
Moreover, as part of the preferred qualifications, students must be enrolled in a full-time degree and plan to return to their studies after completing the internship.
Candidates attending a degree program in the United States will be preferred.
Similarly, they should have previous research experience through internships, full-time roles, or research labs.
Students should have experience contributing to academic research communities, including publishing in recognized conferences or journals.
They should also have proficiency in at least one programming language, such as Python, Java, JavaScript, C/C++, etc.

Benefits :
If you meet the eligibility criteria above, you must apply for this Google program and work with a top organization. Keep reading to know about the benefits this research program offers all applicants.
Benefits of Google PhD Student Researcher Program:
All students will gain practical experience of working with a top organization.
Google offers paid internships so students do not have to worry about financial constraints.
Similarly, the internship offers flexibility in terms of length, time commitment and onsite/remote work nature.
Candidates will work across teams working in research, engineering, and science roles.
It is a fantastic opportunity to become a Google Student Researcher.

Google Student Researcher Requirements:
Updated CV/Resume
Current official/unofficial transcript

Application Process:
Do you want to know how to apply for Google Student Researcher PhD? Click the ‘apply now’ button and start your application process.
Before you apply, make sure you are eligible and meet all requirements for this internship for PhD students.
Fill out the application form and submit the necessary documents.
Applicants can share their preferred working location from the locations available.
Make sure you apply before the deadline.
Google PhD Student Researcher Deadline:
The deadline to apply for the Google PhD Student Researcher Program 2025 is
