Eligibility Criteria :
Are you enthusiastic about photography? If you are, you must apply for Deloitte’s Photo Grant 2025. However, before you apply, review the eligibility criteria below to ensure you meet all the requirements and are eligible for these photography grants.
Eligibility Criteria for Deloitte Photo Grant 2025:
The photography contest is open to all amateur and professional photographers from around the world.
Applicants must be under 35 years of age to participate in this award.
Similarly, submitted work must be original, unpublished works that have not previously received awards or been showcased in other events or competitions.
Each photographer is allowed only one entry and can submit only a single project idea.
AI-generated images are permitted as long as the description clearly mentions that the visuals were created using artificial intelligence.
Moreover, all projects and ideas must follow the theme for the year 2025.

Benefits :
Do you meet the eligibility criteria and requirements above? If yes, you would not want to miss the Deloitte Photo Grant Award. Continue reading to know more about the benefits of participating in this competition.
Benefits of Deloitte Photo Grant:
It is a paid competition that offers winners a chance to win cash prizes.
The selected winner will receive a grant of €20,000 to carry out their proposed photography project.
The completed project will also be presented as an exhibition during the 2026 edition of the award.
Moreover, the winner of this competition will have the opportunity to exhibit their work at Triennale Milan from November 2025.
They will also get travel and accommodation in Milan. Hence, applicants do not need to worry about financial constraints.
It is a fantastic paid photography competition that you should not miss.
The Deloitte Photo Grant offers visibility with international and known jurors.
All photographers will gain exposure for their work and ideas.

Application Process:
Want to know how to apply for the Deloitte Photo Grant 2025? Click the ‘apply now’ button below to start your application for the open call.
Before you apply, make sure you meet all the requirements of this photography award.
All applicants must submit a project idea.
This should include 1500 characters project description, a budget estimate, an image portfolio of ten images and any other necessary documents that may be required.
Make sure to apply before the deadline for the open call.
