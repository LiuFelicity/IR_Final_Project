Eligibility Criteria :
Do you want to learn the Korean language for free? Then apply for the Korean course after checking your eligibility given below. There are not many prerequisites, but there are some criteria for this free Korean course with certificate.
Eligibility Criteria of the Online Korean Language Free Course:
Individuals must have internet to study from this Yonsei University Korean Language Program free.
You must have a strong will to learn the Korean language.
People from all over the world (any country) can register for a free online Korean language course.
There are no prerequisites as the course is perfect for beginners.
There are no restrictions for these free Korean courses; people from any nation can enroll.

Benefits :
The free Korean language is full of benefits, especially for beginners. Opportunities circle has mentioned the various benefits and financial coverage of the First Step Korean Course by Yonsei University below.
Benefits of the Free Online Korean Language Course:
This online course is self-paced; you can register and start learning according to your schedule.
The free online Korean language course has flexible deadlines.
After completing the Korean Language course, students will gain knowledge about the Korean alphabet, communicate in the Korean language and learn Korean culture.
You can also receive a free certificate by applying for financial aid on the platform. Therefore, the option for financial aid on Coursera is available for learners.
It is one of the best free online Korean courses.

How to Apply for Financial Aid for the Korean Language Course Certificate?
Applicants can fill out the form for financial aid/support, and then access this course free of cost and receive the certificate.
Select the course “First Step Korean” to apply for financial support.
Click on NEXT and then click on the continue to the application.
Submit your financial aid application, keeping in mind the minimum word count that is 150 words.
Wait for 16 days for financial aid application to be reviewed.

Application Process:
The application process for this course is free and online. With this korean language course online free course, you can learn Korean Language.
Register for the free Korean language course by following the ‘Apply Now’ button below.
Before you enroll for the course, apply for financial aid to receive a free certificate.
Select the option ‘enroll for free’ and sign in with your Email account.
You can also apply for financial aid by providing the asked information.
After accessing the financial aid, you will get free access to the course and a shareable certificate too.
This is an excellent opportunity to learn Korean language. If you are looking to work or study in South Korea, this free online Korean course will be extremely helpful.
There is no deadline to apply.
