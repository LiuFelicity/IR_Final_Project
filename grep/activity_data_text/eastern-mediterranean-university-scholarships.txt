Eligibility Criteria :
Do you want to study in Turkey on a scholarship? If you do, make sure to apply for this graduate scholarship and also go through the eligibility criteria for Eastern Mediterranean University Scholarships in Turkey 2024-2025 below.
Eligibility Criteria for Eastern Mediterranean University Scholarships 2024-25:
Applicants from all countries in the world can apply for this scholarship in Turkey.
For master’s programs conducted in English and all Ph.D. programs (offered in both English and Turkish), proof of English language proficiency is mandatory.
If applicants do not possess a valid English language qualification that meets EMU’s requirements, their English proficiency level will be assessed through an examination conducted by EMU.
Those applying for a PhD program, must have a Master’s degree.
Eligible candidates for the EMU scholarships Turkey must possess a minimum Bachelor’s degree with a CGPA of 3.00 out of 4.00 or its equivalent.
Moreover, departments will rank applicants based on their assessment criteria, which includes factors beyond CGPA.
International students can apply for 100% and 50% scholarships for PhD programs and 50% scholarship in Master’s programs.
Similarly, Turkish citizens can apply for both 100% and 50% scholarships for Master’s and PhD programs.
Students registered as special or visiting students are not eligible for the EMU Scholarship in Turkey.

Benefits :
If you meet the eligibility criteria mentioned above, then keep reading about the various benefits the Eastern Mediterranean University Scholarships offer. Opportunities Circle has compiled a list below.
Benefits of Eastern Mediterranean University Graduate Scholarships:
EMU graduate scholarships provide 100% and 50% scholarships to students. Hence, providing financial assistance and  covering a portion or all of the tuition fees.
Scholarship recipients will gain access to a high-quality education at EMU, known for its diverse academic programs and experienced faculty.
EMU Graduate Scholarships often recognize and reward academic excellence and outstanding achievements.
It is an excellent opportunity to experience the Turkish culture.
Being part of the EMU community provides access to a vast network of alumni, which can prove beneficial for future career opportunities.
For international students, EMU Graduate Scholarships offer the chance to study in Turkey and gain international exposure.

Documents Required for EMU Scholarship 2024-25 in Turkey:
Official transcripts
Attested copies of previous diplomas
Letters of reference (3)
Statement of Purpose/Intention
Copy of Passport or ID card
A passport-size photograph
Proposal (for PhD program)
English/Academic Proficiency test scores

Application Process:
Wondering how to apply for the Eastern Mediterranean University Scholarship for international students?
To apply, click the ‘apply now’ button below or visit the official website. All applicants must visit the online application portal.
Before you apply. ensure you meet the EMU Scholarship requirements.
Moreover, if you have not created an account, log on to the EMU online application portal.
EMU Scholarships are available to students enrolling in their respective programs for the first time. Students who have terminated or been dismissed from their registration and are reapplying are not eligible.
Make sure to submit all necessary documents as mentioned above.
Applications for these scholarships in Turkey are submitted during the online program application process.
