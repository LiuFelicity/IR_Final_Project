Eligibility Criteria :
Are you interested in studying human behavior? If so, you will certainly want to take advantage of this exceptional opportunity to study Social Psychology for free. Before you enroll, however, be sure to carefully review the eligibility requirements for this online course.
Eligibility Criteria of Introduction to Social Psychology Course:
Since this course is in English, a good command of the language is necessary.
The online course is for people with an interest in Psychology. However, people with any background can apply.
No prior knowledge or skills are necessary for this Social Psychology free course on edX.
Moreover, there are no country restrictions, people from all around the globe are eligible to apply.
Applicants need a computer or device with internet access to participate in this online course.
People wanting to enroll in this course by the University of Queensland must allocate one to two hours weekly as an essential requirement.

Benefits of the Social Psychology Free Online Course 2023:
As an online course, it offers flexibility in terms of when and where participants can study, accommodating diverse schedules and commitments.
The course encourages participants to reflect on their own behavior and beliefs, hence leading to greater self-awareness and personal growth.
