Eligibility Criteria for Morgan Stanley Technology Internship 2025:
Applicants should currently be enrolled in a BSc (or above) in Computer Science, Engineering, Mathematics, Physics, or a related field.
Similarly, interns should be proficient in English language, both written and spoken.
Interns should also have strong problem-solving and communication skills, along with a teamwork mindset.
They should be familiar with at least one object-oriented programming language such as Java, C++, C#, or .NET.
