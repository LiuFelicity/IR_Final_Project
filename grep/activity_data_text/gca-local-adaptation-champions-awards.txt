Eligibility Criteria :
Does this innovative award sound interesting to you? If it does, make sure to thoroughly review the eligibility criteria for the GCA Champions Awards.
Eligibility Criteria for the GCA Local Adaptation Champions Awards 2024:
Women-led groups, Non-governmental organizations (local, national, and international), Universities, research institutes, think tanks, Governments, Entrepreneurship support organizations, Micro, small, and medium enterprises (MSMEs), Tech and non-tech enterprises, Local corporates or organizations promoting local climate adaptation along supply chains are eligible to apply for this international award.
Applicants from all over the world can apply.
The entry must fit into one of the four GCA Awards categories.
Applicant must meet all of the following criteria to be eligible for the 2024 GCA Local Adaptation Champions competition:
The intervention is being implemented, and the results are demonstrable.
The intervention aims at the most vulnerable communities, society, and individuals experiencing climate impacts.
The intervention addresses adaptation to climate change impact or builds resilience against climate impacts.
The intervention is locally led, adhering to one or more Principles of Locally Led Adaptation.
