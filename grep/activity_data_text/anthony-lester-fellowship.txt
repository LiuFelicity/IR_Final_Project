Eligibility Criteria :
If you want to pursue a law fellowship, you must apply for the Lester Fellowship Program. Before you apply, go through the eligibility criteria below to ensure you meet all requirements.
Eligibility Criteria for Anthony Lester Fellowship 2025:
Anthony Lester Fellowships are open to candidates from diverse geographic locations.
The fellowship is open to early-career lawyers and law students.
The project must aim to create a measurable impact in promoting human rights or the rule of law.
Moreover, it should involve practical fieldwork rather than academic or theoretical research.
The project must be feasible, manageable, and realistic in scope.
Similarly, the work should take place in a country other than the applicant’s own, offering them a unique and transformative professional experience.
Applicants should undertake the project at a point in their career where it can significantly influence their professional development.
At the end of the fellowship in USA, fellows will have to give a presentation Bard’s campus in New York.
They will also write a brief report about their project, which Bard College’s Human Rights Project will publish on its website.

Benefits :
Do you meet the eligibility criteria above? If you do, you would not want to miss these paid fellowship in USA. Keep on reading if you want to know more about the benefits this law fellowship offers.
Benefits of Anthony Lester Fellowship:
All fellows will receive a stipend of $25,000 for a project with a minimum duration of three months.
At the end of the Anthony Lester Fellowships, fellows will present their project findings at Bard College’s campus in New York, with travel and accommodation costs fully covered.
Similarly, fellows will write a brief report on their project, which will be published on the Human Rights Project’s website. They may also opt to publish their work elsewhere, if they acknowledge the support received from the Anthony Lester Fellowships at Bard College.
Fellows will also engage in discussions about their work with professionals who collaborated with Anthony Lester at the Odysseus Trust, an organization that supported his legislative initiatives.
It is an excellent fellowship for law students and lawyers from around the world.
