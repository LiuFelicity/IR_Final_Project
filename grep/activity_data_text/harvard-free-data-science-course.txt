Eligibility Criteria :
Do you want to become a data scientist? If yes, you must apply for the Harvard Data Science Courses. However, before you apply, go through the eligibility criteria and requirements for this free online course.
Harvard Data Science Course Eligibility:
The free data science course is open to everyone from around the world.
It is an introductory-level course, so there are no prerequisites or degree requirements.
Similarly, this Harvard Course has no age restrictions, people of any age are welcome to apply.
Candidates should be interested in learning data science, data analytics, data visualization and programming.
All those applying should have an up-to-date browser so that programming is possible directly in a browser-based interface.
The data science course at Harvard University is free. However, applicants need to pay a certain amount to upgrade to be eligible for a certificate.
Moreover, applicants must invest 1-2 hours weekly to complete this course.

Benefits :
Do you meet the eligibility criteria above? If you do, then you should not miss this opportunity to learn the basics of data science. Go ahead and read the various benefits these free courses by Harvard offer.
Benefits of Harvard Free Data Science Course:
The course is completely self-paced, candidates can study and complete the course according to their own schedule.
This online free course is an introductory level course with no background requirements.
Harvard free course will help applicants learn the basics of data science through data visualization.
They will also learn other highly demanded skills such as data analysis, programming, R, ggplot2, data statistics and so on.
In addition, learners will receive a shareable Harvard certificate after completing this course and choosing the verified track.
It is one of the best data sciences courses, and interested students must apply.
