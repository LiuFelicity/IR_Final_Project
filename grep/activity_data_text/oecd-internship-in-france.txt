Eligibility Criteria :
Do you want to become an OECD intern? If you do, then make sure to apply for the internship by OECD. However, before that, you must review the eligibility criteria for OECD France Internship below to ensure you meet the requirements.
Eligibility Criteria for OECD Internship 2025 in France:
Students who are currently studying a degree program accredited by a recognized institution, including Bachelor’s, Master’s, or PhD programs, throughout the entire duration of their internship, are eligible to apply for the International OECD internship 2025 in France.
Applicants must be currently enrolled full-time in a degree program that is directly related to the work carried out by the OECD.
Most internships are located in Paris, France, and are offered to nationals of OECD member countries. However, opportunities are also available to individuals from non-OECD member countries for specific programs.
Interns must have an excellent command of at least one of the two official languages of the OECD, which are English and French. Proficiency in multiple languages used at the OECD is considered advantageous.
Moreover, interns at OECD must commit to participating in the internship in Paris for a minimum period of one month.
Strong quantitative and IT skills are an essential requirement for the OECD internship programme 2025.
Similarly, all interns are expected to possess excellent drafting and communication skills.
Applicants should also demonstrate the ability to work collaboratively in a multicultural and international environment.

Benefits :
If you meet the eligibility criteria above, make sure to apply for the OECD Internship 2025 and also read the various advantages of this internship in Europe.
Benefits of OECD Internship in Paris, France:
The Organization for Economic Co-operation and Development Internship offers interns a monthly living allowance, paid as a contribution to monthly living expenses of around 1,000 Euros.
There is no registration fee and applicants can easily apply for the OECD Internship 2025 in France.
This internship offers a valuable opportunity for professional growth by providing hands-on experience.
Interns at the OECD work in a multicultural and international environment.
In addition, OECD internships can be conducted remotely or in a hybrid format if approved by the hiring team.
It is an excellent opportunity to work in Europe and pursue an internship in Paris, France.
The OECD internship program is a fantastic paid internship in France for international students 2025.

Documents Required for OECD Internship Programme 2025 in France:
Details of bank account
Copy of passport or ID
Proof of full-time enrollment at a university or academic institution
Internship contract signed by your institution
Proof of medical insurance
Copy of visa (if applicable)

Application Process:
Want to know how to get an OECD Internship in France 2025? If you want to know about the OECD internship application, keep on reading.
To apply for the OECD Internship Programme,  visit the official application portal or click the ‘apply now’ button below.
However, before you apply, ensure you meet all the requirements and eligibility criteria for the Organization for Economic Co-operation and Development internship.
The application process is online and interested applicants should carefully fill out the application form and submit all the necessary documents.
Moreover, the OECD Internship 2025 accepts applications all year round on an on-going basis.
Applicants should submit their applications two to three months before the intended start date.
The selection process includes submission of online application and cover letter addressed to the Internship Office.
The Internship Office reviews all applications and shortlists suitable interns, forwarding them to OECD Directorates.
Similarly, hiring teams may request candidates for a telephone interview or written test.
Upon successful completion of the interview or written test, selected candidates will receive an internship offer letter via email from OECD Human Resources.
OECD Internship 2025 Deadline:
There is no deadline to apply for the OECD Internship Programme 2025 in France.
