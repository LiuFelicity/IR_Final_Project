Eligibility Criteria :
Are you interested in studying in Italy? Then why not apply for the University of Pavia Scholarships 2026? Before visiting the official website and applying, check the criteria and requirements to ensure your eligibility.
Eligibility Criteria for CICOPS Scholarships in Italy 2026:
Applicant must be a citizen of a Developing country (classified by the World Bank).
They should have at least two years of teaching experience or activity within international organizations and institutions.
Open to both junior and senior members of universities.
Individuals who have an interest in the field of development studies are encouraged to apply.
Individuals currently enrolled in any doctoral or postgraduate program at an Italian University are not eligible.

Benefits :
CICOPS Scholarship is a fully funded scholarship that covers all costs related to studying abroad. This is an excellent chance for researchers to receive a fully funded scholarship in Italy. Details are given below.
Benefits of the CICOPS Scholarship Program 2026 in Italy:
It is a fully funded scholarship.
Travel expenses will be covered.
Meals will be provided.
Accommodation expenses will be covered.
Health insurance and personal accident insurance will be provided.
Participants will get a stipend of 150.00 Euros per week.
Access to all the necessary facilities for study and research activities (libraries, computer facilities, etc.).
Scholars will get a visa invitation letter.
Opportunity to have study and research experience in Italy.

CICOPS Scholarship Requirements:
Curriculum Vitae  (ideally in European format, including a list of publications)
An invitation letter from the university professor or researcher with whom the applicant wants to collaborate

Application Process:
The application process for the CICOPS Scholarships in Italy 2026 is online.
Applicants are requested to obtain a letter of invitation from their contact faculty member at the University of Pavia.
After that, they are required to fill out the online application form available at the official website.
Fill out the application form carefully and submit it with all the required materials.
Applicants who are unable to obtain a letter of invitation should contact CICOPS. These applicants will receive a research proposal form from CICOPS, which they must fill out and send back to
[email protected]
.
Then the CICOPS team will review their research proposal.
Make sure to submit a complete application because an incomplete application will be rejected.
Results will be made by 30th June 2025.
CICOP Scholarship Deadline:
The last date to apply for the University of Pavia CICOPS Scholarships 2026 in Italy is 31st May, 2025.
