Eligibility Criteria :
Before starting the application, students interested in applying for the Reach Oxford Scholarship in the UK shall check the criteria and requirements below. The
Reach oxford scholarship requirements
are listed below.
Eligibility Criteria for Reach Oxford Scholarship in UK 2025:
Applicants must be nationals of one of the countries mentioned above (countries who receive official development assistance from DAC (Development Assistance Committee) of the Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD).
Students must demonstrate a strong academic history.
Moreover, applicants demonstrating the need for financial assistance and engaging in community services will be preferred.
Applicants who have never been a student in any undergraduate program will be considered.
Students who have lack of ability to study undergraduate degree in their own country will also be considered.
Applicants should return to their home country after completing their degree.

Benefits :
All set to start the application? Check what benefits these University of Oxford scholarships for international students are offering you. We have listed the financial coverage of the Oxford Reach Scholarships 2025 below.
Benefits of the University of Oxford Reach Scholarship 2025:
Grant for living cost of international students.
Full tuition fee will be covered through these Reach Oxford Scholarships for Developing Country Students.
One return air ticket every year until the course is completed.
The Reach Scholarship at Oxford University is an opportunity to pursue an undergraduate degree in the UK.
Explore England and its high-quality international education system.

Application Process:
The application process for Reach Oxford Scholarship 2025 in UK is online.
How to Apply for Reach Oxford Scholarship 2025 for Developing Countries:
Ensure that you apply to an undergraduate program at the University of Oxford via UCAS by
15 October 2024
and are successful in securing admission.
Details about the scholarship application will be available on the official website on 15 January 2025.
Students can submit their scholarship application after securing admission.
Fill out the Oxford Scholarship 2025 form and attach all supporting documents.
An application confirmation email will be sent to the students.
University of Oxford Scholarship Selection Criteria:
Motivation for the selected course.
Academic record.
Applicants’ commitment of returning to their home country
Reach Oxford Scholarship
Deadline:
The last date to apply for an undergraduate degree at the University of Oxford is 15 October 2024.
The last date for the University of Oxford Reach Undergraduate Scholarship is 12 February 2025. Applications for these undergraduate scholarships will open on 15 January 2025.
The selection process will take place in March 2025, and the results of the application for these Oxford University scholarships for international students will be announced in April 2025.
