Eligibility Criteria :
If you are interested in getting this fully funded internship in Japan, before you start the application, check the eligibility criteria and requirements of the HENNGE’s Internship 2025 in Japan to ensure your eligibility.
Eligibility Criteria for HENNGE’s Global Internship 2025 in Japan:
Applicants must be pursuing a bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or any related field or have equivalent experience from any tech internship or bootcamp.
Third-year students or higher can apply.
They should be fluent in English.
You should have knowledge of Linux, MacOS, or Unix-like systems.
They should be interested in open source or tech community activities.
Applicants should also have experience in Python or Go for Full Stack Pathway or React or Vue in TypeScript for Front-End Pathway only.
Moreover, they should be interested in building a career in Japan.
Familiar with AWS and Terraform tools.
Hands-on experience in full-stack web development.
You should have a basic understanding of server-side programming for front-end roles.

Benefits :
You must be thinking about what benefits this HENNGE’s GIP 2025 will provide to international students.  Opportunities Circle has listed the HENNGE’s Internship benefits and financial coverage below.
Benefits of the HENNGE’s Global Internship in Japan 2025:
Interns will get a monthly subsidy.
They will also get pre-arrival support and visa guidance
Round-trip airfare will also be provided.
Non-Japanese interns will get medical insurance (for non-Japan residents)
They will also get Japanese mobile with data.
Students will also get additional perks such as networking luncheons, monthly technical sessions, office facilities, refreshments, and staff parties.
Interns will also get access to Japanese cultural events.
An amazing opportunity to visit Japan for free.

Application Process:
The application process for HENNGE’s Global Internship in Japan is online.
Click on the Apply Now Button below to visit the official website.
On the official website, select the internship of your choice and register yourself.
Fill in the HENNGE Registration form; after that, you will need to complete this coding challenge.
Applicants must submit their work along with their CV and cover letter.
Shortlisted applicants will be contacted for a phone screening and an online interview.
There are limited internship slots, and positions are filled on a first-come, first-serve basis.
Make sure to apply as early as possible to secure your position before applications close!
HENNGE Internship Deadline:
The Japan internship program does not have any predefined deadline; the program has some specific slots that work on a first-come, first-serve basis. So don’t miss this chance and apply now to secure your spot.
