Eligibility Criteria :
If you want to study in the UK with a fully funded scholarship, don’t miss this British Council Stem Scholarship 2025. However, before you start the application process, check the eligibility criteria for the British Council Scholarships 2025.
Eligibility Criteria of the British Council Scholarships for Women in STEM 2025 in UK:
Applicants must be women from the participating countries mentioned above
to be eligible for these fully funded Scholarships for Women in Stem 2025.
They must be available from September 2025, as the academic year starts in September/October.
Furthermore, applicants must demonstrate their need for the scholarship to be eligible.
They must hold an undergraduate degree to pursue a master’s degree.
Moreover, applicants must be proficient in English to be eligible for this British Council scholarship.
Dual nationality holder students are not eligible to apply for these British Council Women in STEM scholarship.
Applicants must be active in the field of interest with work experience or a proven interest in their subject area.
They should not be receiving financial support in the UK from any other source.

Benefits :
You must be thinking about the benefits of this scholarship and what expenses will be covered by the host organization. Opportunities circle has listed the benefits of the British Council Scholarships for Women in STEM 2025.
Benefits of the British Council Women in STEM Scholarships 2025-26:
The scholarship in UK 2025 will cover full tuition fees and other study expenses.
Moreover, students can study at their desired universities in the UK.
Living stipend will be provided for the whole scholarship duration.
These British Council Stem scholarships will also cover the student’s travel costs.
Visa expenses and health coverage will be covered under these STEM scholarships for females in UK.
Some universities will cover the IELTS fee as well.
Furthermore, the Women in Stem Scholarships 2025 provide special support for mothers.
The British Council Scholarship for Women in STEM is a fantastic opportunity to study and live in UK.
