Application Process:
The enrollment procedure is online. Applicants must create an online account following the steps below:
Enter their email address.
Enter their name; remember, this name will be used for your certificate.
Create a public username.
The username cannot be changed.
Create a password and add your country.
At last, click Create an Account.
Now, you can choose your desired course and click enroll.
Once enrolled for any free course, you can immediately start the UC Berkeley free online course!
There is no deadline for the University of California free online courses; you can enroll anytime.
