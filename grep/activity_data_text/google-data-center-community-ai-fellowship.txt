Title: Google Data Center Community AI Fellowship 2025 | Fully Funded - Opportunities Circle

Financial coverage:
Fully Funded

Deadline:
June 22, 2025

Eligibility Criteria :
If you want to become a Google fellow, you must apply for the Data Center Community AI Fellowship. However, before doing so, go through the eligibility criteria below to check if you meet the requirements.
Eligibility Criteria for Google Data Center Community AI Fellowship:
Applicants must be at least 18 years old to apply.
They must be based in or creating impact within a Google Data Center Community located in the USA, Finland, or the UK.
Similarly, ideal candidates are founders, entrepreneurs, or community leaders using AI and technology in their ventures.
The venture should be in the Problem-Solution or Product-Market Fit stage.
Fellows should have a strong community connection and a proven history of driving positive change.
Candidate’s non-profit or for-profit organization should have an established business model and is actively serving customers or beneficiaries.
Moreover, all applicants must fully participate in the 16-week fellowship.
They should attend a virtual immersive from 1 September to 12 September (ten days of programming).
AI fellows should also join weekly virtual workshops every Tuesday, Wednesday and Thursday starting from 15 September.

Benefits :
Do you meet the eligibility criteria above? If you do, you would not want to miss these online fellowships. Keep on reading if you want to know more about the advantages of this Google AI Fellowship.
Benefits of Google Data Center Community AI Fellowship:
It is a fully funded fellowship generously supported by Google.
Applicants will gain practical knowledge on using AI and technology to address local challenges.
Chance to receive expert training and guidance from experienced mentors and industry leaders.
Moreover, it is a fantastic opportunity for skill enhancement and leadership growth.
Fellows will become part of a network of purpose-driven entrepreneurs and leaders
They will design and lead a Basecamp community event with expert guidance and also receive a stipend for it.

Application Process:
Want to know how to apply for the Google Data Center Community AI Fellowship? Click the ‘apply now’ button below and start your application.
Before you apply, make sure you meet all the requirements of this fully funded fellowship.
The admissions committee will evaluate all applications.
Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an online interview.
Final decisions will be announced by 15 July and the program will begin on 1 September 2025.
Make sure to apply before the deadline.
