Eligibility Criteria :
Do you want to become an NSTC intern? If yes, you must apply for these paid internships. However, before applying, go through the eligibility criteria for NSTC International Internship Pilot Program in Taiwan.
Eligibility Criteria for NSTC International Internship 2025:
NSTC fellowship in Taiwan is available to students from all around the world.
This Taiwanese internship is open to bachelors, masters and PhD students of foreign nationality who are studying in foreign colleges or universities.
Moreover, foreign nationals of Mainland China, Hong Kong and Macau are not eligible for this program.
All applicants must be focused on key research and development areas in Taiwan, including humanities and social sciences.
Similarly, international students who have completed exchange programs in Taiwan and have not yet returned to their home countries can also apply for NSTC Internship Pilot Program.
Applicants must be proficient in one of the following languages, English, Mandarin, or Taiwanese.
Language proficiency levels must be indicated during application and relevant proof documents must be uploaded.

Benefits :
If you meet the eligibility criteria above, do not forget to read the various advantages of these internships in Taiwan. Opportunities Circle has compiled a list of benefits below.
Benefits of NSTC International Internship Pilot Program in Taiwan:
There are no age restrictions to apply for the NSTC Internship.
It is a fantastic paid internship opportunity for students.
NSTC fellowship in Taiwan is offering all interns a subsidy of NT$1,000 per day for a maximum duration of three months.
Furthermore, students will be able to advance their studies or start their career in Taiwan in the field of Science and Technology.
All interns will work on NSTC research projects with their project leaders (PIs). Hence, gaining new knowledge and increasing the chances of getting employment in Taiwan.
The 2025 NSTC International Internship Pilot Program in Taiwan is an excellent program to study in Taiwan and experience Taiwanese culture.

Documents Required for NSTC Internship in Taiwan:
Complete application form
Curriculum vitae/CV
Certificate enrollment
Head-shot photo
Letter of recommendation

Application Process:
Do you want to know how to apply for NSTC International Internship in Taiwan?
To apply for this paid internship, click the ‘apply now’ button below which will redirect you to the official application portal.
NSTC interns will have to register on the online portal with their email and password
Make sure to submit all the necessary documents as mentioned above.
Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis. All interns must ensure they meet the eligibility criteria and requirements for the NSTC Internship Program in Taiwan.
IIPP will select 600 interns. However, applications may be closed earlier if the quota is complete.
Moreover, applicants can start their internships at any time between June 2025 any December 2025.
Make sure to apply for the IIPP 2025 before the deadline.
NSTC Taiwan Internship Deadline:
The deadline to apply for the NSTC International Internship in Taiwan is
15 September 2025
(Deadlines may vary according to the batch).
