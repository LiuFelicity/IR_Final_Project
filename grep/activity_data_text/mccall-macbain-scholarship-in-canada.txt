Eligibility Criteria :
Are you ready to study for free in Canada as an international student? Then check your eligibility before you start preparing your documents and application for the McCall MacBain Scholarship in Canada for international students.
Eligibility Criteria for the McCall MacBain Scholarships 2026 in Canada:
Applicants can be Canadian citizens or international students who are eligible for the fully funded scholarship for international students.
Furthermore, applicants should be less than 30 years old.
They must be current students on track to get their first bachelor’s degree by August 2026.
Applicants must have earned their first bachelor’s degree in the past five years (till January 2020 or later).
Moreover, international students must pursue their studies full-time.
Applicants must have to pass an English proficiency test.
The program at McGill must have 45 credits or more to be eligible for the McCall MacBain Scholarship Program.

Benefits :
Before starting the application process, read the benefits of the McGill University Scholarships in Canada for international students. The McCall MacBain Scholarship 2026 in Canada is a flagship scholarship at McGill. Opportunities circle has listed the benefits and financial coverage of this international scholarship in Canada below.
Benefits of the McGill University McCall MacBain Scholarship 2026:
There are a total of 10 McCall MacBain scholarships for international students, 25 international finalist awards of $20,000, and 15 regional awards of $10,000 for international students.
Applicants will get a full tuition fee for the duration of their degree.
Students will also receive a 2,300 CAD monthly stipend.
Applicants will get a one-time relocation grant for moving to Montréal.
Students who will not pursue an academic term during the summer break may apply for summer funding up to $5,000 to pursue unique and meaningful summer experiences.
Scholarship recipients will also get a chance to connect with international advisors and mentors, build community and attend different workshops and talks with this McGill Scholarship.
An amazing experience and opportunity to study for free in Canada as an international student and become a McCall MacBain Scholar.

McCall MacBain Scholarship Requirements:
2 References (one academic and one professional)
Official or Unofficial transcript
One-page resume

Application Process:
You must be thinking about how to apply for the McGill-MacBain Scholarship in Canada 2026.
The process to apply for the McCall MacBain Scholarship is very simple. Click on the
Apply Now
button given below, and make an account on the official website to start your application process.
At the start of the application, applicants must provide personal information about themselves and their family members in the application form.
Then applicants should share their community activities, work history, extra-curricular activities, and any breaks that they have taken.
After that, they are required to write a 250-word short essay describing their future goals and activities.
Applicants must upload their resume and official or unofficial transcript.
To attach the reference letters, applicants have to submit a reference request; after submitting the request, your mentioned referees will get an email attaching a link to submit their reference letter.
Applicants should submit all the details and documents carefully before the McCall MacBain Scholarship deadline.
After the scholarship application, applicants must apply separately to eligible programs at McGill University in September for the following fall semester.
After selection, applicants will appear in regional and final interviews.
McCall MacBain Scholarship 2026 Deadline:
The application deadline for the McCall MacBain Scholarship in Canada for international students is
20 August 2025.
The application deadline for the McCall MacBain Scholarship in Canada for Canadian and U.S. applicants is
24 September 2025
