Eligibility Criteria :
Want to learn graphic designing? If yes, you must apply for the Adobe Illustrator Free Course and have the chance to polish your skills. However, before you apply, make sure to go through the eligibility criteria and requirements below.
Eligibility Criteria for Adobe Illustrator Free Course:
The introduction to Illustrator Course is open to everyone from around the globe.
There are no age requirements or prerequisites for this course.
It is an introductory-level course and requires no previous experience.
Moreover, this free course is ideal for beginners or individuals aiming to transition into a Graphic Design career.
Applicants will have to assign one to two hours per week to complete this LCIEducation course.
Learners should also have access to the internet and a device to access this course.
Similarly, candidates can audit the course for free. However, they will have to pay a fee to earn a certificate.

Benefits :
Do you meet the criteria and requirements of this designing course? If you do, you must not miss this opportunity to learn the basics of Adobe Illustrator software. Keep on reading to know about the benefits of enrolling in this graphic design course.
Benefits of Adobe Illustrator Free Course:
The online illustrator course is a beginner-level course perfect for those looking to learn the basics of designing.
All applicants will study at their own pace and access the course online for free through the audit option.
Similarly, learners will master the basics of Adobe Illustrator, the top tool used by professionals in graphic design, branding, UI/UX and more.
They will learn how to design custom logos, icons, diagrams and other visual content.
It is a fantastic opportunity to upskill or prepare for roles in graphic design and related fields.

Application Process:
Want to know how to learn Adobe Illustrator for free? Apply for this Adobe Illustrator Free Course by clicking the ‘apply now’ button below.
