Eligibility Criteria :
If you are a researcher looking for funding, you must apply for the Royal Academy of Engineering Research Fellowship. However, before you apply, go through the eligibility criteria below to make sure you meet all the requirements.
Eligibility Criteria for Royal Academy of Engineering Research Fellowships 2025-26:
Applicants must be an early-career researcher with a PhD that was awarded no more than four years before the deadline.
A three-month extension beyond the four-year limit may be allowed.
There are no nationality or age restrictions. Applicants from around the world can apply.
Moreover, Research Fellowships must be hosted at a UK higher education institution/university or a UK research organization eligible for UKRI funding.
The host institution must provide the fellow with the same level of support as a permanent employee.
Similarly, the proposed research must be within the field of engineering.
The host institution is also responsible for obtaining necessary work permits and covering related costs.
Applicants who previously applied but were unsuccessful may reapply.
Fellows must start their fellowship between 1 August 2026 and 31 October 2026.
Applicants must also secure formal approval from their host institution before applying.

Benefits :
Do you meet the eligibility criteria above? If yes, you would not want to miss this fellowship in UK. Keep reading the advantages of the Royal Academy Fellowship that Opportunities Circle has compiled below.
Benefits of Royal Academy of Engineering Research Fellowships:
The UK fellowship is capped at a maximum contribution from the Academy of £625,000 over a five-year period at 80% of full economic costs. With the host institution responsible for the remaining amount.
Fellows receive mentoring support from an Academy Fellow to support research and career growth.
They will have lighter teaching and administrative responsibilities to allow more focus on research.
Similarly, fellows will have access to specialized training programs and additional funding opportunities.
Applicants will also have the chance to connect with other Research Fellows and Academy Fellows.
The paid fellowship in UK will help establish a strong research portfolio, increasing the chances of securing further funding and expanding research teams.
It is a fantastic fellowship for researchers that also offers membership in the RAEng Awardee Excellence Community.

Application Process:
Wondering how to apply for Royal Academy of Engineering Research Fellowships 2025? Click the ‘apply now’ button below to start your application.
Before you apply, make sure you are eligible and meet the requirements.
Applications must be submitted through the Academy’s Grants Management System (GMS).
The program has a three stage selection process, including a general review, expert review and interviews.
Fill out the application form and provide any additional documents if required.
Make sure to apply before the deadline.
