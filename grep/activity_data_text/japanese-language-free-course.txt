Eligibility Criteria :
If you are looking for a Japanese Language Course online, you must apply for this Course by Tsinghua University. However, before you apply, make sure to go through the eligibility criteria below to know you are eligible to apply.
Eligibility Criteria for Japanese Language Free Course 2025:
Applicants from all over the world can apply for this Japanese Language course.
It is an introductory level course and there are no prerequisites.
Similarly, applicants should spend around three to five hours per week to complete this Course.
All applicants must have a device and a stable internet connection to access the edX course.
There are different ways to enroll. If applicants wish to enroll for free, they should select the audit course option. However, if they want to get a certificate, they must pay a fee.
Moreover, there are no age requirements to apply for these online language courses.

Do you meet the eligibility criteria above? If yes, you would not want to miss this opportunity to learn a new language. Continue reading to know about the benefits of this Japanese Language Free Course.
Benefits of Japanese Language Free Course:
The Japanese Language course for beginners is an introductory level course perfect for those who want to master Japanese.
Applicants will understand key aspects of Japanese traditions and lifestyle through real-life scenarios.
They will gain essential vocabulary and conversational abilities for everyday interactions.
Moreover, applicants will develop Japanese language skills for basic communication.
It is one of the best Japanese Language Courses.
