Eligibility Criteria :
Are you looking at how to improve your communication and public speaking skills? If yes, this course will be an excellent opportunity for you to do so. Before you apply, however, make sure you go through the eligibility criteria for this free online course on edX.
Eligibility Criteria for RIT Free Course 2024:
People from all across the globe can apply for this free speaking course.
The Public Speaking online course is open to individuals from all backgrounds.
There are no prerequisites or prior knowledge requirements for this online course by the Rochester Institute of Technology.
All those choosing to enroll in this course will have to allot four to six hours per week.
All applicants must confirm that their computer or device meets any technical requirements specified by the course provider, such as compatible web browsers, software, or plugins.

Benefits :
If you meet the eligibility criteria above, keep on reading the various benefits of the Public Speaking online course by the Rochester Institute of Technology in 2023.
Benefits of the Public Speaking Course by RIT:
Learners will gain the ability to apply the core elements of the speech communication process, enabling them to communicate effectively and confidently.
The online course by RIT is self-paced. Hence, individuals enrolled can set their own schedules with ease.
Participants will explore a variety of tools and methods for managing and controlling anxiety associated with public speaking. Empowering them to deliver presentations with composure.
They will gain in-depth knowledge and hands-on experience related to communication and public speaking.
Learners will develop the skill to create well-structured and impactful speeches. Thus, mastering the art of engaging an audience and effectively conveying messages.
Moreover, the course will enable participants to critically evaluate and provide constructive feedback to other communicators, applying the principles and techniques covered in the course.
Participants will sharpen their ability to identify central themes and supporting points in both their own speeches and those of others. Hence, enhancing overall communication clarity and comprehension.

Application Process:
How to Apply for the Public Speaking Online Course?
Click on Apply Now button below to start your application for Free RIT Speaking course.
To apply, start by visiting the official website, the link of which is provided below.
Individuals have the option to choose the session they want to enroll in.
Applicants can opt for the free session by clicking on the Audit track, which does not require any registration fee.
However, the course can be upgraded to a verified track by paying $199 as a registration fee.
RIT Free Course Deadline:
There is no last date mentioned for the Rochester Institute of Technology Free Public Speaking Course.
