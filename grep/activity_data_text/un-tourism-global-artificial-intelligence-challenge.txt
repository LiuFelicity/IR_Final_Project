Eligibility Criteria :
If you want to participate in this UN program 2025, you must apply for the UN Tourism Artificial Intelligence Challenge. However, before you apply, go through the eligibility criteria below to make sure you meet all requirements.
Eligibility Criteria for UN Tourism Global Artificial Intelligence Challenge 2025:
The challenge is open to individuals, teams and companies from around the world.
Participants must be of legal age and capable of entering into a contractual agreement.
Applicants must submit a comprehensive proposal including their innovative solution, its impact on the tourism sector and a clear implementation plan.
Proposals will be assessed based on their creativity, feasibility and potential for scalability.
Moreover, initiatives should contribute meaningfully to achieving selected sustainable development objectives.
Participants of the UN Global Challenge should show openness to forming partnerships for scaling and impact.
Proposals must have the capacity to make a significant impact on the tourism sector.
Evaluation Criteria:
Scalability
Preparing for partnerships
Ease of value capture
Potential impact on business
Product/technology maturity
Maturity of the team/organization
Contribution to selected Sustainable Development Goals

Benefits :
Do you meet the eligibility criteria above? If you do, you must apply for the UN Tourism Global Artificial Intelligence and Deep Tech Challenge. Keep on reading to know more about the benefits this international challenge offers.
Benefits of UN Tourism Global Artificial Intelligence Challenge:
Winners will gain exposure by presenting their pitches at the UN Tourism Demo Day.
They will also receive a two-month incubation period at SPARK Innovation Sphere.
Similarly, winners will have access to more than 90 venture capitalists.
The UN Global Artificial Intelligence Challenge offers a fantastic opportunity to engage with leading companies and over 150 countries.
Winners will also receive personalized mentoring programs and the possibility of a pilot project with collaborators from private sector companies and institutions.
Moreover, startups will have a profile on Playbook, an invitation to participate in the Silicon Valley Summit and access to talent and synergies within the Plug and Play Portfolio.
It is a fantastic UN program for people and startups focusing on the tourism sector.

Application Process:
Wondering how to apply for the UN Tourism Global Artificial Intelligence Challenge?
Before you apply, make sure you meet the challenge terms and conditions and ensure all requirements are met.
Select the category most relevant to your project.
Create a clear and impactful pitch deck in English, with a maximum of 15 slides.
After finalizing all documents, complete and submit the application form
Finalists will be announced in April 2025.
