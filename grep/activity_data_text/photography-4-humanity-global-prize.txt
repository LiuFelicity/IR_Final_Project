Eligibility Criteria :
Are you a photographer looking for photography competitions? If yes, you must apply for the 2025 Humanity Global Prize. However, before you do, review the eligibility criteria to ensure you meet all the requirements of this Global Prize contest.
Eligibility Criteria for Photography 4 Humanity Global Prize 2025:
Participants must be at least 18 years of age or older to enter.
The photographs must relate to people being impacted by climate change or people trying to reverse or mitigate climate change.
The entry must include the photographer’s name, title, location, date, and description of each photograph submitted.
The photographs must have been taken between 1 August 2024 and 5 August 2025.

Benefits :
If you meet the eligibility criteria above, you will not want to miss these photography contests. Continue reading to learn more about the benefits of the 2025 Global Prize Competition.
Benefits of Photography 4 Humanity Global Prize 2025:
The 2025 Global Prize winner will receive $5,000 USD.
The Global prize competition recipient, top 10 finalists, and top 20 Honorable mentions will get an opportunity to be exhibited globally via UN.org.
It is an amazing opportunity for photographers to showcase their skills on an international platform.

Application Process:
The application process for the Photography 4 Humanity Global Prize 2025 is online.
Click on the Apply Now button to visit the official website.
Applicants are required to register themselves on the official website.
After that, they will be able to submit their photographs for the 2025 Global Prize Competition.
The entry must include the photographer’s name, title, location, date, and description of each photograph submitted.
Make sure to submit your entry carefully before the deadline.
2025 Global Prize Competition Deadline:
The last date to apply for the Photography 4 Humanity Global Prize 2025 is
05 August 2025.
