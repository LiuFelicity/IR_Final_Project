Eligibility Criteria :
Are you interested in pursuing a career in the scientific field? If you do, you must apply for this amazing encouragement award. Before you proceed, make sure to read the eligibility criteria and requirements below.
Eligibility Criteria for Science & SciLifeLab Prize for Young Scientists 2025:
Students from all over the world are eligible to apply.
They must have received their Ph.D. between 1 January 2023 and 31 December 2024.
The work they will submit in their application must have been performed by students in their graduation.
Applicant’s thesis work must be related to one of the qualified categories.

Benefits :
If you meet the eligibility criteria above, you should definitely apply for this international award by Science & SciLifeLab. Also, go through the various benefits. Opportunities Circle has listed the advantages for you below.
Benefits of the Science & SciLifeLab Prize for Young Scientists 2025:
The international prize will select four winners.
The grand winner will get a prize money of 30,000 USD.
The other three winners will get a prize money of 10,000 USD each.
It is an amazing encouragement opportunity for young students.

Science & SciLifeLab Prize Requirements:
Essay (1000 words)
One-page reference letter
List of citations and awards
Relevant professional experience
Thesis Abstract

Application Process:
The application process for the Science & SciLifeLab Prize for Young Scientists 2025 is online.
Click on the
Apply Now
button below to visit the official website.
Applicants are required to fill out an online entry form.
Applicants are required to submit an essay describing their thesis work in one of the eligible categories.
The essay must not exceed 1,000 words and may include one figure or table.
Applicants are also required to submit a one-page reference letter from their advisor, professor, or any member of the thesis committee with an assessment and comment on the applicant’s research.
Make sure to upload all the required documents.
Review your application and submit it before the deadline.
Science & SciLifeLab Prize Deadline:
The last date to apply for the Science & SciLifeLab Prize for Young Scientists 2025 is
15 July 2025.
