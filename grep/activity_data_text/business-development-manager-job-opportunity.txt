Eligibility Criteria :
Here are the criteria for Business Development Manager:
Candidates must be residing in Islamabad.
They must have good interpersonal and leadership skills.
Candidates must be good at decision-making and working individually.
Candidates must have good team management skills.
Furthermore, they must be proficient in both English writing and spoken English.
Proficient in Word, Excel, Outlook, and PowerPoint.
Good planning and organizing skills.
Ability to meet tight deadlines.
Candidates must be able to communicate their ideas.

Job/Internship Description :
Candidates must proactively identify and respond to the needs of students and/or educational institutions.
They must be able to gather, disseminate and share best practices.
Work with clients to solve their problems using education theory.
Be able to present your ideas and solutions in an effective way.
Use, develop and support assistive technologies.
Work to a professional code of ethics and remain impartial.
Your responsibilities will also often include organizing or facilitating events and workshops.
Developing teaching and learning materials.

Benefits :
Here are the benefits of working with Opportunities circle:
The organization will provide a good stipend/salary.
Opportunity to work with an international youth organization.
Exposure to the professional working environment.
Gain work experience.
Improve your networking by working with an international organisation.
